  • Welcome to Soho Square Athens,
  • the newest addition to an established network with offices around the world
  • including New York, London, Milan, Moscow, Sao Paolo and Shanghai.
  • Soho Square is part of WPP, the largest communications services group in the world.
  • Soho Square exists to help its clients compete successfully and increase their sales.
  • Soho Square provides multi disciplinary solutions in every form of marketing communication.
  • Too many agencies find it impossible to deliver truly integrated,
  • coherent campaigns because their thinking is dominated by a single discipline.
  • Hence their response to a brief is too often
  • dictated by their capability rather than what the brand really needs.
  • Put simply, most agencies have a constrained way of doing things,
  • and more often than not, it results in work that is limited and generic.
  • At Soho Square we reject the usual way of doing things.
  • We believe that the approach of many conventional advertising agencies
  • is generic and outdated and certainly not integrated.
  • We work with a genuinely open mind,
  • using whatever talent is necessary to take advantage of every opportunity.
  • This gives us the freedom to apply a more liberated style of thinking
  • which we call "Wise Radicalism"
  • Our approach goes well beyond the usual realms of advertising and communications,
  • but to us, taking a holistic view of your business
  • is the best way to get the job done properly.
  • Our first and most important duty is to understand and take a holistic view of a client's business
  • to help them solve strategic business issues not just communications ones.
  • However big or small the task,
  • our work always complements strategic business requirements.
  • Wise Radicalism dictates that we look at every situation
  • on its own merits and do whatever is necessary to be most effective.
  • We never ever start with a predetermined solution in mind.
  • It combines innovative thinking with common sense and experience
  • in order to make a tangible difference to the choices
  • and purchase decisions consumers and opinion formers make every day.
  • Soho Square is a genuinely multi-discipline integrated agency
  • containing multi-skilled individuals with an open-minded,
  • assume-nothing approach to solutions.
  • Soho Square is full of people with multiple skill-sets acquired
  • from different communications backgrounds including:
  • advertising, design, digital, sales and marketing.
